# Privacy Policy for MusicScoodle Students

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025

## Introduction
MusicScoodle Students ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- Name and contact information
- Student ID and school affiliation
- Lesson schedules and attendance records
- Progress tracking data
- Communication with teachers

### Usage Data
- App usage statistics
- Device information
- Log files and crash reports

### Camera and Media
- Photos for lesson documentation (with permission)
- Audio recordings for practice (with permission)

## How We Use Your Information
- Provide and maintain our service
- Manage lesson scheduling and tracking
- Communicate with teachers and schools
- Improve app functionality
- Send important notifications

## Data Sharing
We do not sell personal information. We may share data with:
- Your assigned teachers and schools
- Service providers (with data protection agreements)
- Legal authorities when required by law

## Data Security
We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including encryption and secure servers.

## Your Rights
- Access your personal data
- Request data correction or deletion
- Opt-out of communications
- Data portability

## Children's Privacy
Our service is designed for students of all ages. For users under 13, we comply with COPPA requirements and obtain parental consent.
